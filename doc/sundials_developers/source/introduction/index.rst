Introduction
============

The SUite of Nonlinear and DIfferential/ALgebraic equation Solvers (SUNDIALS)
consists of the CVODE, ARKODE, KINSOL, and IDA as well as the CVODES and IDAS
variants with sensitivity analysis capabilities.
